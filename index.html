About Us

Kartika.id is a community initiative founded by Ugi Fitri Syawalyani, an Industrial Engineering student at Universitas Hasanuddin and an alumna of the Young Leaders for Indonesia program by McKinsey & Co. Our mission is to empower female engineering students—starting from Eastern Indonesia and now expanding nationwide. We aim to bridge the gap in support, opportunity, and access to networks through mentoring programs, career-sharing sessions, and inclusive community building. Kartika.id helps female engineering students navigate career paths in engineering, break stereotypes, and grow both professionally and personally.

Our Missions

Our mission is to empower future female engineers by providing them with the necessary tools, knowledge, and confidence to excel in the engineering field. We believe in fostering an environment where women can thrive and break barriers.

We aim to build a strong, supportive network for female engineering students and professionals. Through Kartinection, we connect members with industry leaders, alumni, and peers, creating valuable opportunities for collaboration and growth.

We are dedicated to the personal and professional development of our members. Our programs, including Kartishare and Kartiship, are designed to enhance skills, expand knowledge, and guide individuals through their career journeys in engineering.
